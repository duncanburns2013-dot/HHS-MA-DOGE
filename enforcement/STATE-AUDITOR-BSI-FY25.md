# MA STATE AUDITOR — BUREAU OF SPECIAL INVESTIGATIONS, FY25

Auditor Diana DiZoglio's Bureau of Special Investigations (BSI) is the formal MA channel for public-benefit-fraud referrals across **MassHealth, Department of Transitional Assistance (DTA), and Department of Early Education and Care (EEC)**. This is one tier closer to enforcement than the State Auditor's general performance-audit work and runs in parallel with the AG's Medicaid Fraud Division.

---

## FY25 results (fiscal year ending 6/30/2025)

- **$11,952,288** in public-assistance fraud identified
- **4,179** fraud investigations completed
- Largest single program: SNAP — over **$4.1M** identified
- MassHealth (Medicaid): over **$1.3M** identified
- **Source:** [Mass.gov BSI FY25 announcement](https://www.mass.gov/news/auditor-dizoglios-bureau-of-special-investigations-identifies-nearly-12-million-in-public-benefit-fraud-in-fy25)

## March 2026 follow-up release

- Additional **$4.4M+** in public-benefit fraud identified
- **Source:** [Western Mass News coverage](https://www.westernmassnews.com/2026/03/13/study-finds-over-44m-massachusetts-public-benefit-fraud/)

## Programs in BSI's jurisdiction

- **MassHealth** — Medicaid-funded healthcare (overlap with every entity in this repo)
- **DTA** — TANF, SNAP, EAEDC
- **EEC** — early-education subsidies

## Why BSI matters for this repo

The MASTER-SUMMARY notes the State Auditor "found EOHHS has no documented process" for rate calculation under 101 CMR 350.04. That finding was a Performance Audit; the BSI is the same office's investigations arm, and it is *actively* identifying MassHealth fraud at the case level. The structural critique in this repo (markup math, two-layer pass-through) and the case-level evidence BSI surfaces are complementary — and BSI accepts referrals.

## Auditor's broader compliance push

DiZoglio is also litigating to compel the Legislature to comply with audits, including over alleged $12M in unspecified fraud / NDA misuse — the audit-NDA case is currently pending before the SJC.
