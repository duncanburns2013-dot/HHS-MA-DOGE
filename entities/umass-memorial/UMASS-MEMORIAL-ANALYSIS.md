## 🔴 CRITICAL FINDING: THE CEO IS A REGISTERED LOBBYIST

**Eric W. Dickson, MD** — CEO of UMass Memorial Health Care — is personally registered as an **in-house lobbyist** with the Massachusetts Secretary of State every year from 2021–2025. He lobbies on "all matters related to hospitals, health care and health care financing."

Simultaneously, Dickson made **$13,688 in personal political donations** to 28+ recipients including the Governor, Lt. Governor, Speaker, Senate President, House Ways & Means Chair, and MHAPAC.

On some OCPF filings, Dickson listed his occupation as **"Lobbyist."**

This is the only entity in this investigation where the CEO is both a registered lobbyist AND a direct campaign contributor to the officials he lobbies.

## 4. THE TAXPAYER SUBSIDY QUESTION

UMass Memorial Health Care operates as the teaching hospital system for **UMass Chan Medical School** — a division of the **University of Massachusetts**, which is a state-funded public university.

The relationship means:
- State capital funding flows through UMass system
- MassHealth (Medicaid) is a massive revenue source
- State 340B drug pricing benefits flow through the system
- Public university affiliation provides research grants, training programs, and institutional prestige

Yet this taxpayer-connected institution spends **$618K+ on state lobbying** and its employees make **$356K in political donations** — including the CEO registering as a lobbyist while earning $3.9M.
